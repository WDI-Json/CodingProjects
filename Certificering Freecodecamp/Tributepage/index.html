Some information about Netsky:

Netsky (pronounced as: Ëˆnet-skai'), the artist name of Boris Daenen, born in Edegem on the 22nd of march 1989 in Belgium. Known as a DJ and producer of Drum & Bass music.

His artistname is named after a computervirus roaming around in the period he started making music. The idea behind it was people downloading the virus when looking for illegal music.

The name on itself is an anagram of the computernetwork Skynet from the Terminator-saga.
